The Launch of Aurora

In 2023, Sofia Williner achieved a remarkable feat by igniting the entrepreneurial world with her candle business, "Aurora," which quickly soared to unprecedented heights. Hailing from Argentina, Sofia's passion for crafting exquisite candles combined with her entrepreneurial spirit led to the creation of a brand that transcended borders. What began as a small, locally-adored venture soon became a global sensation, attracting a devoted following worldwide. Sofia's dedication to crafting candles of unparalleled quality, using sustainable and eco-friendly materials, resonated with customers seeking both elegance and ethical consumption.


Sofia's relentless pursuit of excellence and her innovative approach to candle-making positioned "Aurora" as a frontrunner in the industry. Her business prowess, coupled with her unwavering commitment to sustainability and social responsibility, made her not only a trailblazer but also a role model for young women aspiring to break barriers in entrepreneurship. At an impressively young age, Sofia Williner demonstrated that with vision, dedication, and a dash of creativity, it's possible to build a thriving global business that leaves a positive impact on both customers and the planet, earning her the title of the youngest woman to achieve such a monumental feat.
